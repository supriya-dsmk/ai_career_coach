My Weekly Schedule
==================

I work a standard 9 AM to 5:30 PM job, Monday through Friday.

Weekday routine:
- 9:00 AM - Standup meeting (15 min)
- Rest of the morning is usually heads-down coding
- 12:00-1:00 PM - Lunch break (I usually eat quickly and have about 20 minutes free)
- Afternoons: mix of meetings and coding. Tuesdays and Thursdays tend to be heavier on meetings
- 5:30 PM - I'm usually done for the day

Available time I can realistically use for learning:
- About 10-15 minutes during lunch break most days (I browse my phone anyway)
- 30 minutes after work on Monday and Wednesday (before dinner)
- Saturday mornings I have about 1-2 hours free before family activities start
- Sunday evenings I sometimes have 30-45 minutes before winding down for the week

Honestly, I'm pretty busy during the week. The most consistent slot is probably
that 10-minute lunch break window. I'd love a plan that makes the most of small
pockets of time rather than requiring big blocks I can't commit to.
