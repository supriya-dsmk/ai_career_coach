## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Priya is a high-performing Senior Engineer with exceptional technical depth (4.3/5) and delivery track record (4.0/5). Her promotion to Staff Engineer is blocked not by technical ability but by a **visibility and influence gap** — the single most common barrier at the IC4→IC5 transition. Four of five peer reviewers independently flagged that Priya's impact is significantly greater than her perceived impact, and critically, Priya herself does not recognize this deficit. This plan directly addresses that blind spot while honoring her strengths.

**Weekly (every Friday, 10 min — add to brag document):**
1. What did I make visible this week that I would previously have kept to myself?
2. Did I speak up in every meeting where I had a relevant perspective? If not, what held me back?
3. What did I delegate or enable someone else to do? What did I resist delegating, and why?
4. Who outside my immediate team did I interact with meaningfully this week?

**Monthly (end of Weeks 4, 8, 13 — 20 min deeper reflection):**
1. If I were my manager writing my IC5 promotion case right now, what would be the strongest evidence? What would be the weakest?
2. Look at my brag document: am I capturing enough? Am I using impact language or just listing activities?
3. What conversation am I avoiding that I know would help my growth? What's the cost of continuing to avoid it?
4. Am I falling back into old patterns (doing everything myself, staying quiet, not sharing credit/wins)? What triggered the regression?
